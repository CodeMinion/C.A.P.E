About:

Smart Editor for creating metadata of comics panels with the goal of providing a frame by frame experience in the Comic Hero viewer app.

  • Process: Drop a comic page or folder to have the editors computer vision algorithms analyse and extract its panels.
  • Add Panel: Hold down (Control or Command) and press Left Click to create a new panel.
  • Edit Panel: Edit any existing panel by selecting it and dragging it's handles.
  • Delete Panel: Left click a panel to delete and press the Delete key.
